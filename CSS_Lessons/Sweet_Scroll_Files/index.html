Strawberry Cheesecake

If you enjoy the sweetness and the slight bitterness of strawberries, you may want to give this recipe a try! It is a wonderful treat for fruit lovers who need a special treat. Make it to serve guests, as a dessert, or a simple snack!

EQUIPMENT LIST

  • Springform pan
  • Blender
  • Saucepan
  • Electric Mixer
  • Wrie cooling rack
  • Utensils

INGREDIENTS

  • Cream Cheese
  • Sugar
  • Sour Cream
  • Strawberries

Steps

  • Make the graham cracker crust by combining cream, cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Pressing the crumb mixure in the bottom and up the sides of your springform pan. Then baking in a 350 deg.F oven for 10 minutes. Then set aside to cool.
  • Make the Creamy cheesecake filling by beating the cream cheese with an electric mixer until smooth and creamy. Beat in the sugar, sour cream, salt, and vanilla until well combined. Gemtly stir in lightly beaten eggs until well combined.
